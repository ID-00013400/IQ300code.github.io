OUR MISSION

Projecting of a complex of structures for aerodromes,heliports and
helipads (artificial runways, taxiways and main tracks, parking areas and aprons),
engineering communications of aerodromes, airfield lighting systems, air navigation facilities.
Projecting of transport infrastructure complexes, engineering networks and systems,
development of design estimates for the reconstruction and overhaul of public highways, main roads, city streets and various container sites.
